Salem to Kerala Tour Package in Famous Place

---------- ----------

Kochi

Kochi (Cochin) is a vibrant city in Kerala, known for its rich cultural heritage, colonial architecture, scenic waterfronts, and unique blend of old and new. It’s a perfect starting point for a Kerala tour.

  • Visit the Chinese Fishing Nets, one of the most iconic sights of the city.
  • Marine Drive is a scenic promenade along the backwaters of Kochi. You can take a leisurely walk here or enjoy a boat ride to get views of the city’s skyline and surrounding waters.

Alleppey Boat House

Alleppey (Alappuzha) is one of the most famous destinations in Kerala for experiencing the traditional houseboat cruises on its serene backwaters. These houseboats offer a unique way to explore the lush landscape, charming villages, and tranquil waters, providing a peaceful retreat amidst nature.

The houseboats are equipped with modern amenities, including comfortable bedrooms, a living area, a dining space, and a private bathroom. Some even come with a sun deck where you can relax and enjoy the view.


Varkala Beach

Varkala Beach is a stunning coastal destination in Kerala, known for its natural beauty, cliffs, and relaxed vibe. It’s a popular spot for both tourists and locals, offering a mix of scenic views, spiritual experiences, and recreational activities.

Best Time to Visit Varkala:
  • The best time to visit Varkala is between October and March. During this period, the weather is pleasant, making it ideal for beach activities, sightseeing, and outdoor exploration.
  • The monsoon season (June to September) brings lush greenery, but the weather can be unpredictable with heavy rainfall, which may not be ideal for beach visits.

Athirappilly Water Falls

Athirappilly Waterfalls, often referred to as the "Niagara of India," is one of the most stunning and popular waterfalls in Kerala. Located in the Vazhachal Forest Range of the Thrissur District, Athirappilly is a must-visit for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and anyone looking to experience the natural beauty of Kerala.

And the best way to go there is to book Kerala holiday tour packages with Rainbow Call Taxi.

Salem to Kerala tour package covering in 4-5 Days.


  • Day 1- Depart from Salem in the night by taxi (approximately 7-9 hours travel). Reach Kochi in the Morning. Check-in to your hotel.

    • Relax and take a leisurely stroll to experience the sunrise and around Marine Drive or visit the Fort Kochi, Vypin Island and traditional Kerala Kathakali performance area enjoy the local culture.

  • Day 2- Kochi to Alleppey (about 1.5 to 2 hours drive).
    1. Experience a traditional houseboat cruise through the scenic Alleppey backwaters. Enjoy the views of lush landscapes, paddy fields, and local villages while savoring delicious Kerala cuisine onboard.
    2. Stay overnight on the houseboat or in a resort near the backwaters (if you prefer staying on land).
  • Day 3- Alleppey to Varkala.
    1. Visit the Janardana Swamy Temple, a 2,000-year-old temple, and take in the spiritual atmosphere.
    2. Check into your hotel or resort, then spend the rest of the day at Varkala Beach. You can relax by the beach or take a walk along the cliffs with beautiful views of the Arabian Sea.
    3. Enjoy a peaceful sunset and explore the local cafes along the cliffs.
  • Day 4- Varkala to Athirappilly Waterfalls.
    1. Explore the stunning Athirappilly Waterfalls, one of the largest in Kerala. You can take a walk around the falls or simply relax and enjoy the beauty of the surroundings.
    2. Visit the smaller but equally beautiful Vazhachal Waterfalls nearby.
  • Day 5- You will reach Salem, concluding your memorable Kerala trip.

In God’s own country, Kerala, Munnar is heaven on earth, being the best hill station in the Western Ghats. And the best Munnar tour are only with Rainbow Call Taxi for years now. Munnar has a mesmerizing landscape set on the banks of three streams.

With lush green tea plantations and waterfalls, Munnar is the best place for all nature lovers. Munnar Tour from Salem by Rainbow Call Taxi is the best way to enjoy nature at its best.

The Munnar tour from Salem with Rainbow Call Taxi can rejuvenate anyone. And it is the reason that every year, many of our clients visit Munnar with our Munnar tour packages for family.

Places to Visit in Munnar

  • Eravikulam National Park : A sanctuary home to the endangered Nilgiri tahr, a mountain goat
  • Mattupetty Dam and Lake : A scenic spot with boat rides, activities, and the Indo Swiss Dairy Farm
  • Attukal Waterfalls : A scenic waterfall with a narrow wooden bridge
  • Kolukkumalai Tea Estate : A high-altitude tea plantation with great views
  • Pothamedu View Point : A view point with tea, coffee, and cardamom plantations, plus treks and hiking
  • Kundala Lake : A lake where you can take a Kashmiri-Shikara boat ride

Wayanad

Wayanad is a beautiful district in Kerala, known for its lush green landscapes, misty hills, wildlife sanctuaries, waterfalls, and rich cultural heritage. Located in the Western Ghats, Wayanad is a popular destination for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life.

Wayanad is a perfect destination for anyone looking for a peaceful retreat in nature with a mix of adventure, wildlife, and scenic beauty.

Places to Visit in Wayanad

  • Edakkal Caves are famous for their ancient petroglyphs (rock carvings) that date back over 6,000 years.
  • Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve and is home to a wide variety of wildlife, including elephants, tigers, leopards, and many species of birds. It’s an ideal place for wildlife enthusiasts and photographers.
  • Pookode Lake is perfect for a peaceful day of boating and relaxation. The area around the lake is also ideal for short treks and picnics.
  • Sentinel Rock Waterfalls Soochipara is a stunning three-tiered waterfall surrounded by dense forests. It’s an excellent spot for nature lovers and photography enthusiasts.
  • High Wavy Mountains, Meghamalai offers some of the most scenic views in Wayanad. It’s less crowded and ideal for those looking to enjoy nature in peace, with vast tea plantations and forested areas.
